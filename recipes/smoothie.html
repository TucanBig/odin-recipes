Smoothie

A picture of a random smoothie, and it looks worse than mine

Description

This is a smmothie that I make everynight, while im not on a bulk nor a cut I drink this as my protein shake. And sometimes when I don't have protein I make it without it because the ingredients it comes with still make it taste good

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of assorted fruits, usually from costco
  • A good amount of milk
  • Good amount of cinnamon
  • Chia seeds
  • 1 Nanner
  • 2 Scoops of your favorite protein

Steps

  1. Pour milk into blending bottle
  2. Pour desired amount of chia seeds
  3. Pour 2 scoops of your protein
  4. Pour your cup of fruits and nanner
  5. Pour your cinnamon
  6. Blend and enjoy
