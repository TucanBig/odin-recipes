Burger

A juicy burger that doesn't beat mine but still taste good

Description

One of the city’s best new burgers comes from this hip, homey, and all-around badass spot led by Fort Worth chef Stefon Rishel. Nestled in the South Main area, the restaurant offers avant-garde odes to American classics, including a stunning house burger, nicknamed “The Flynt.”

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb beef patty
  • Carmalized onions
  • Seasame buns
  • Butter
  • Pepperjack cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Thousand Island

Steps

  1. Season burger with whatever seasong you want
  2. Cook burger on high stove for 6 minutes each side
  3. Grill onions with butter
  4. buns with butter on each side
  5. Put all condiments on then be happy
